UW's MyPlan Professor Rating Chrome Extension

R

Enhancing Course Selection with Professor Ratings Integrated into MyPlan. This project streamlines the process of choosing the right professors by integrating data directly from RateMyProfessors into the University of Washington’s MyPlan platform. Using real-time data, it highlights key metrics such as professor ratings, difficulty levels, and student satisfaction (“Would Take Again” percentages). By providing this information at the point of course selection, the extension helps students make more informed academic decisions. The goal is to simplify registration and offer data-driven insights into professor quality without the hassle of navigating between different websites.

College Football Predictive Analysis

R

Data-Driven Analysis of NCAA Division 1 Football Metrics for Performance Insights. Using advanced data analysis techniques, this project merges multiple datasets to explore key performance indicators in college football. By examining offensive and defensive rankings, rushing and passing metrics, and special teams efficiency, the project aims to identify trends and factors that contribute to winning games. The goal is to provide actionable insights that challenge conventional wisdom, emphasizing the significance of offense and punt return efficiency over traditional defensive metrics.
